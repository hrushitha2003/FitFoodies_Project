Sweet Potato and Black Bean Bowl:

Ingredients: Sweet potatoes, cubed Black beans (canned or cooked) Corn kernels Avocado, sliced Cherry tomatoes, halved Red onion, finely chopped Cilantro, chopped Lime juice Cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper to taste Quinoa or brown rice.

Instructions: Roast sweet potatoes with olive oil, cumin, paprika, salt, and pepper until tender. In a bowl, combine black beans, corn, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and cilantro. Mix in roasted sweet potatoes and squeeze lime juice over the bowl. Serve over quinoa or brown rice, and top with sliced avocado...
