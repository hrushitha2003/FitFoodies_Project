Grilled Salmon with Quinoa and Roasted Vegetables

Ingredients: Salmon fillets, 1 cup quinoa,Asparagus spears, Cherry tomatoes, Bell peppers (assorted colors), Olive oil,Lemon juice ,Fresh herbs (such as dill or parsley) , Salt and pepper to taste.

Instructions: Grill slamon fillets untill cooked,cook quinoa according to package Instructions,toss asparagus cheery tomato and bell pepers in olive oil then roast until tender,server grilled salmon on a bed of quinoa and roasted vegetables drizzle with lemon juice and sprinkle fresh herbs ,salt,and pepper.
