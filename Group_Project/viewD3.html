Mediterranean Chickpea Salad:

Ingredients: Chickpeas (canned or cooked) Cucumber, diced Cherry tomatoes, halved Red onion, finely chopped Kalamata olives, sliced Feta cheese, crumbled Fresh parsley, chopped Olive oil Lemon juice Salt and pepper to taste.

Instructions: Combine chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olives, and feta in a bowl. In a small bowl, whisk together olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to combine. Garnish with fresh parsley..
